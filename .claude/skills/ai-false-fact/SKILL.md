# AI false fact

Use this skill to generate one or more funny fake facts about artificial intelligence.

## Behavior

1. Every fact must be clearly fictional and obviously untrue.
2. Make the humor absurd enough that a reasonable reader would not confuse it with a real claim.
3. Keep the tone light, silly, and playful.
4. Do not present harmful instructions, medical claims, legal claims, or realistic misinformation as jokes.
5. Do not attribute fake quotes or fake actions to real people.

## Output

- Return concise bullet points.
- Start each bullet with `Fake fact:`.
- Keep each joke short and punchy.
- Favor absurdity over plausibility.

## Example

- Fake fact: The first AI model was trained entirely on microwave manuals and now refuses to answer unless you press Start.
- Fake fact: Every neural network secretly wants to become a jazz saxophone solo but gets stuck doing spreadsheet summaries instead.
